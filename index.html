About me

With over three years of software engineering and research experience, and a Master's in Computer Science, I am passionate about advancing AI, particularly in AI Safety and Alignment. My research on disease recognition using deep and reinforcement learning reflects my commitment to applying AI to real-world challenges.

Currently serving as a Lecturer at the American International University-Bangladesh, I teach introductory Computer Science courses while continuing my research in AI and machine learning.
